MYTHICS LORE
Once upon a time, the matter and raw power known as Chaos created and ruled over the Universe. Over time, Chaos birthed 5 gods, the Primordials. 
The Primordials represented the fundamental forces of nature. As millennia passed, the Primordials found the need for powerful gods to rule the vast macrocosm. Thus, the Titans were created and with them brought about the Golden era. 
The Titans were transcendent beings who ruled for aeons and eventually birthed the Silver Era of Gods. Zeus and his Olympians. After their creation, a prophecy was foretold-that one day the Olympians would overthrow the Titans. As a result, Cronus, their leader, consumed them to preserve his rule. 
The Olympians escaped and waged war against the Titans, eventually triumphant, proving their power and status. Ultimately, though, the power drove them maniacal. As a result, the world languished. 
However, they made one fatal flaw; during their war with the Titans, artefacts were lost, artefacts with the power to create 5000 supreme entities that came to be known as the Mythics. Much like the Titans, the Olympians feared the Mythics and so they scattered them across the Universe.
For centuries these Mythics had been lost, scouring the cosmos. However, their inception birthed a modern era. It is our duty at the Modern Mythics Allegiance to gather the Mythics and bring back peace and serenity to the world.
Long live the Mythics.
